# Hello World: The Beginning of Aura

Every garden starts with a seed.

This is the first post on **Aura** — a digital garden where I'll be exploring the intersection of design, code, and the quiet beauty of restraint.

## Why a Digital Garden?

Traditional blogs are linear. Posts are dated, categorized, and filed away. But ideas don't work that way. They grow, branch, intertwine. A digital garden better reflects how thoughts actually evolve.

Here, you'll find:
- **Writings** on design, code, and the spaces between
- **Projects** I've built, broken, and rebuilt
- **Notes** — half-formed thoughts that may grow into something more

## The Aesthetic

This site is built around a simple idea: **glass and light**. Glassmorphism isn't just a visual trend — it's a metaphor for transparency, depth, and the way ideas refract through different contexts.

Every element here uses `backdrop-blur`, gradient borders, and subtle shadows to create that sense of looking through layers. It's not decoration — it's the point.

3. Building design systems that breathe
4. The art of digital restraint

Stay tuned. The light is just getting started.
